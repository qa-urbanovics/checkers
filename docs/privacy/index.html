01 What the app stores ›

The app saves two things to your device's local storage: your preferences (board theme, piece style, sound, language) and your game statistics (wins, losses, total games). These never leave your device.

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app. You can also reset statistics from within the app: Settings → Statistics → Reset.

Local only · Never transmitted
02 No tracking or analytics ›

There are no analytics tools, crash reporters, ad networks, or third-party tracking SDKs in this app. I have no visibility into how it's used, how long sessions last, or anything about the people who play it.

03 No account, no cloud ›

There is no sign-up, no login, no user profile, and no cloud sync. Nothing about you exists anywhere outside your own device.

04 Children's privacy (COPPA) ›

The app is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages. Since no data is collected from anyone, it is fully compliant with COPPA and similar children's privacy regulations by design.

05 GDPR & international regulations ›

Because no personal data is collected, processed, or transferred, there is nothing to regulate under GDPR, CCPA, or any other privacy law. You retain full control of any data stored locally on your device.

06 Limitation of liability ›

The app is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. The developer is not liable for any damages arising from its use. This policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Latvia.

07 Changes to this policy ›

If this policy ever changes in a material way, the date at the top of this page will be updated. Given the app is fully offline with no data collection, significant changes are unlikely.

01 Что хранит приложение ›

Приложение сохраняет две вещи в локальное хранилище устройства: настройки (тема доски, стиль шашек, звук, язык) и игровую статистику (победы, поражения, число партий). Эти данные никуда не отправляются.

Удалить их можно в любой момент, удалив приложение. Статистику также можно сбросить внутри: Настройки → Статистика → Сбросить.
