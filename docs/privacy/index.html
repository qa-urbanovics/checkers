Checkers: Emerald Courts
Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: Checkers: Emerald Courts does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Checkers: Emerald Courts ("the App") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is a local game — it does not require an account, does not connect to any server, and does not collect any personal information.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No purchases or payment information

📱 Local Storage Only

The App saves the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game settings (sound, haptics, board theme, language)
  • Game statistics (wins, losses, total games played)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

🌐 No Internet Connection Required

The App works fully offline. No network requests are made during normal use. No data is transmitted to any server.

🔧 Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

👶 Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. As we do not collect any data, material changes are unlikely.
